Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Sprout Merge ("the Game") is a free-to-play offline mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Game.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The Game works entirely offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, including but not limited to:

Local Storage

The Game saves your progress (game state, settings, and preferences) locally on your device using the browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The Game does not use any third-party services such as analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting, or social media integrations.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The Game is safe for all ages.

Internet Permission

The Game may request internet access for optional features such as checking for updates. No personal data is transmitted during this process.
