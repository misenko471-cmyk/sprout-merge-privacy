Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bonbon Sort ("we", "the app") handles information when you use our mobile puzzle game.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Bonbon Sort is a fully offline game. We do not require registration, login, or any personal information to play. The app does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Photos, contacts, or files
  • Analytics or tracking data

2. Local Storage

The app saves your game progress (current level, coins, achievements, settings) locally on your device using the browser's localStorage mechanism. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Can be deleted by clearing the app/browser data

3. Third-Party Services

The current version of Bonbon Sort does not integrate any third-party services such as advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs.

If advertising (e.g., Google AdMob) is added in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before the release, and any ad-related data handling will be clearly disclosed.

4. Children's Privacy

Bonbon Sort does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The game is suitable for all ages and does not contain any mechanism for data collection.

5. Permissions

Bonbon Sort does not request any special device permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
